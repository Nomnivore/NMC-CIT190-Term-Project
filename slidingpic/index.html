Instructions

Objective

The goal of this game is to rearrange the pieces of the picture back into the correct order. The pieces are arranged in a grid pattern, and each piece can only be moved to the empty space.

How to Play

Click on a piece to move it into the empty space.
If you get stuck, you can click "Reset" to start over.
